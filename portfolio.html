During my student teaching experience at Mount Lebanon High School, I was responsible for teaching Biology to 10th grade students. Additionally, I was responsible for teaching the curriculum used for the Wildlife Biology elective. Below are a few sample lesson plans and evidence of learning from my student teaching experience.

Lesson Name

In this lesson, students dissect a frog to learn the anatomical structure of the amphibian. As part of the lesson, students work in groups of 3-4 students and have a guided lab worksheet with questions to answer along the way. At the end, each student must write-up a lab report that summarizes their findings through the dissection.
