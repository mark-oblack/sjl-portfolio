About

My name is Shannon and I'm currently focused on learning design principles as they relate to web and graphic design. At the moment, I work at a computing Help Desk where I'm primarily responsible for designing any materials used for the department. Some of these materials include flyers to promote upcoming events, newsletter layouts and infographics used in our front-facing location and to communicate through our social media accounts.

While I do not have formal schooling in web and graphic design, I am studying on my own through resources such as Lynda.com, Team Treehouse and freeCodeCamp. I completed a web design certificate in May 2017 and am looking to puruse a Master's Degree in Digital Media Arts down the road.
